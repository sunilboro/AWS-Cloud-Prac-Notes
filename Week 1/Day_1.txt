9. What is DevOps?
Combination of cultural philosophies, pratices, and tools that increases an organization's ability to deliver applications and services at high velocity.

10. DevOps combination of what?
* Cultural philosophies for removing barriers and sharing end-to-end responsibility.
* Processes developed for speed and quality, that streamline the way people work
* Tools that align with processes and automate repeated task, making the release process more efficient and the application more reliable.
